# 🚀 HR & Payroll SaaS - Project Plan

## 📊 Executive Summary

**Project Name:** PayrollPro / HRFlow (pilih nama nanti)  
**Type:** Multi-tenant SaaS Platform  
**Target Market:** Indonesian SMEs (10-500 employees)  
**Tech Stack:** NestJS + Next.js 14 + MySQL + Flutter  
**Timeline:** 12-16 weeks to MVP  
**Budget:** Bootstrap / Self-funded  

---

## 🎯 Vision & Mission

### Vision
Menjadi platform HR & Payroll terlengkap dan termudah untuk perusahaan Indonesia

### Mission
- Otomasi penggajian sesuai regulasi Indonesia
- Simplifikasi administrasi HR
- Transparansi untuk karyawan dan management
- Compliance dengan aturan ketenagakerjaan Indonesia

---

## 👥 Target Users

### Primary Users
1. **HR Manager** (Admin)
   - Kelola karyawan
   - Proses payroll
   - Generate reports
   - Manage attendance

2. **Employees** (Staff)
   - Lihat slip gaji
   - Request cuti
   - Clock in/out
   - Update profile

3. **Finance/Accounting**
   - Review payroll
   - Export accounting data
   - Tax reports

4. **Company Owner**
   - Dashboard overview
   - Analytics
   - Approve requests

## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Compliance with Indonesian Law
**Mitigation:** 
- Hire labor law consultant
- Stay updated with regulations
- Flexible calculation engine

### Risk 2: Data Security
**Mitigation:**
- SOC 2 compliance roadmap
- Regular security audits
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Regular backups

### Risk 3: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on SME market (underserved)
- Better UX than competitors
- Indonesian-specific features
- Competitive pricing

### Risk 4: Technical Debt
**Mitigation:**
- Clean architecture from day 1
- Code reviews
- Automated testing
- Documentation
